Running Rats Toronto

Welcome Back!

We are happy to be able to restart the RunningRats Tuesday runs!

Our new outdoor meet point is at TD Centre Plaza near the NE corner of Wellington and York Streets (look for the cows). Since we are outside, there are no washrooms or bag check.

If you are running with the Rats for the first time, please complete the registration form and waiver here. Plan to arrive at 6:15pm.

Practice standard government recommended Covid precautions, e.g. maintain distance, be aware of your social bubble, do not attend if sick, symptomatic, exposed, under quarantine rules, etc.

Stay safe out there and Run with the Spirit!

We are the Running Rats

Founded in 2002, we’re one of Toronto’s best run clubs. We welcome runners of all abilities, from people training for their first 10K ever, to sub-3-hour Boston qualifiers. We’re a dedicated group who meet every week year-round, but we’re here to have fun – all those weekly miles are more fun with friends.

Drop in any Tuesday and join us!

Run with us every Tuesday!

Join us on our free weekly run! We meet every Tuesday at 6:15pm outdoors at TD Centre Plaza near the NE corner of Wellington and York Streets (look for the cows). We run all year long, rain or shine, with 7K, 10K and 13K options and people running a range of paces every week. We’re a friendly, inclusive group: all ages, genders and paces welcome. All we ask is that you can complete 7K comfortably.
