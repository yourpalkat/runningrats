Rat Tuesday

“Rat Tuesday” is our weekly group run. Anyone is free to drop in and join! Our club is aimed at those who feel comfortable running 7K or more, continuously, at any pace. Each week our rotating crew of run captains choose routes of 7K, 10K and 13K. Our members run at a range of paces, so you’re likely to find someone going your speed and distance. We also have a Moderate Pacer every week who runs at the pace of the last runner, so no one is left behind.

When: Every Tuesday, rain, shine or snow!
New Guests: 6:15pm
Route Call: 6:25pm
Run: 6:30pm sharp!
Where: Decathlon Union Station (inside the new Bay Street Concourse). Look for the entrance at the bottom of the stairs, on the west side of Bay, just south of Front Street. Note that there is no bag check as of yet, though we are told that there will be lockers installed in that concourse at some point.

Second Tuesday of every month: Post-run social!

It’s free to try and free to join! If it’s your first time, please come around 6:15 and introduce yourself: be sure to fill out the sign-up sheet before you come, located here! Run with us three times and you’re a Rat! We’ll add you to the weekly announcement email and our Facebook group so you’ll know about upcoming routes and special events.
