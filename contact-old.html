Important note about COVID-19

Effective March 15 2020, the Running Rats are on hiatus as a result of the ongoing global health crisis. Prolonged exercise in close proximity to others is a potential transmission risk and the Rats are a large group, so in the interest of the health and wellbeing of our community, there will be no weekly run until further notice.

Please read the full text of our announcement here.

We’re on hiatus!

While the pandemic continues, we are not running together – that means no meeting, and no one checking for messages. You can check the homepage of this site for updates; we’ll post more as the situation evolves. We hope to be able to run together again soon – until then, run with the spirit (but safely and distantly)!
