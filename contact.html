Join us every Tuesday!

Join us on our free weekly run! We meet every Tuesday at 6:20pm at Decathlon Union Station (inside the new Bay Street Concourse). Look for the entrance at the bottom of the stairs, on the west side of Bay, just south of Front Street. We run all year long, rain or shine, with 7K, 10K and 13K options and people running a range of paces every week. We're a friendly, inclusive group: all ages, genders and paces welcome. All we ask is that you can complete 7K comfortably.
