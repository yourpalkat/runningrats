Join us every Tuesday!

Join us on our free weekly run! We meet every Tuesday at 6:00pm at the downtown Toronto Running Room, located in the PATH level of the TD Centre at Bay & King. We run all year long, rain or shine, with 7K, 10K and 13K options and people running a range of paces every week. We’re a friendly, inclusive group: all ages, genders and paces welcome. All we ask is that you can complete 7K comfortably.
